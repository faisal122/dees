Deeshen Talent Company

Deeshen Talent Company in association with Film Cafe(Mumbai)aaye hai iss gulistan me....the new super singer from Kashmir for Pa Ma Ga Re Sa 2013-the voice of the future aligned with film production houses from Mumbai and Punjab.The Show has joined hands with Eon films-one of the reputed production houses from Mumbai that will be promoting the career of the winning super talented singer in their project.

The Organisers:DEESHEN TALENT COMPANY

DTC is floated by the young and enthusiastic Parul Kapoor-A Media Personlity and an award winning Choreographer who was bagged a lot of awards in many Dance shows on television as well as choreographed a lot of Punjabi films and videos.A hot young fresh blood with lots of innovative ideas,she floated DTC to promote the talent of this paradise and offer them a platform to reach out to the world at large.Starting off with Pa Ma Ga Re Sa 2014-a singing reality Sensational Show.DTC aims to do a lot of shows in various fields including those in the world of fashion and modelling and aims to change the world perception of Kashmir as not only a visitors paradise but a powerhouse of talent in the world of acting,modelling and singing.

The Logistic Support:EON FILMS

Eon Films has consistently been making feature films in Hindi as well as regional languages including Bengali and Bhojpuri-right from commercial to thought provoking issue based subjects.Based in Mumbai,Eon Films has been in the industry for the last two decades and is spearheaded by Mohan Das-a Director,Producer as well as member of the jury of many a international short film festivals.Having worked with the best of talents in Bollywood.Mohan Das has worked with renowed bollywood celebrities including Manjot Singh,Jaya Bachchan,Zeenat Aman,Farooque Sheikh,Nandita Das and many others.On floors is "Kuch Alagsa" being made for the international festival circuit.
